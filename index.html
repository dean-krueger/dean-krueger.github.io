Hi, my name is Dean! I'm a PhD student in Nuclear Engineering at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research centers on exploring the economic dynamics of nuclear supply chains using CYCLUS, a dynamic, agent-based nuclear fuel cycle simulator. I'm passionate about combining thoughtful analysis with innovative modeling to better understand and shape the future of nuclear energy, energy policy, and the role of nuclear technology in the future of space exploration.
